## Overview

**Lelekwe Farm ERP** is a modern, enterprise-grade farm management system built with **Laravel + Filament**, designed to manage livestock operations, analytics, and farm business processes in one unified platform.

## ---

##  Core Modules

### Livestock Management
- Animal registry with **auto-generated tags**
- Breed classification & prefixes
- Sire/Dam lineage validation
- Lifecycle tracking (Active, Sold, Dead, Culled)

###  Weight Intelligence
- Record animal weights over time
- Detect trends (Gaining / Losing / Stable)
- Bulk PDF weight reports
- Growth analytics dashboard

###  Breeding System
- Controlled breeding records
- Parent validation rules
- Genetic tracking support

###  Health & Treatments
- Medication records
- Treatment history tracking
- Vet logs and notes

###  Sales & Valuation
- Animal sales tracking
- Dispatch workflow (e.g. slaughterhouses)
- Revenue recognition logic

###  HR & Payroll
- Employee management
- Attendance tracking
- Payroll + statutory deductions (PAYE, NSSF, SHA)

###  Reporting & Intelligence
- Executive dashboards
- PDF reports with branding
- QR-based verification system

##  ---

##  Key Features

-  Soft Deletes with Restore (audit-safe)
-  Role-based access (Admin / Manager / Finance)
-  Real-time analytics widgets
-  Premium PDF generation (logos, signatures, QR)
-  Optimized for shared hosting (Hostinger/cPanel)
-  Modular ERP architecture
