## Overview

**Penzi Farm ERP** is a comprehensive farm and livestock management platform built with **Laravel 12, PHP 8.4 and Filament**.

The system centralizes livestock records, breeding, health, laboratory requests, feeding, animal movement, inventory, procurement, sales, accounting, human resources, payroll, reporting and management analytics in one secure platform.

Penzi Farm ERP is designed to improve:

- Livestock traceability
- Farm operational control
- Animal health monitoring
- Financial accountability
- Stock and procurement management
- Management reporting
- Data-driven decision-making
- Role-based accountability
